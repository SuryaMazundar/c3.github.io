How It Works

Mailroom operations, simplified

PackTrack streamlines every step from package arrival to resident pickup in four easy stages.

01
📦

Package Arrives

Staff scan or enter the tracking number. PackTrack auto-fills resident info from your student database and logs the package with type, carrier, and timestamp.

02
🔔

Resident Notified

The system records the check-in and the package is assigned to the correct hall and resident. No spreadsheets, no sticky notes — it's all in PackTrack.

03
✅

Resident Picks Up

When the resident arrives, staff search by name or ID number, confirm their identity, and mark the package checked out in seconds.

04
📊

Full Audit Trail

Every action is logged. Admins can run PO audits, export reports to PDF or CSV, detect and clean duplicates, and review the complete history at any time.

Role-Based Access

The right access for every team member

PackTrack ships with three role tiers that map directly to real residence hall org charts.

🧑‍💼

Office Assistant (OA)

Front-line staff who handle day-to-day mailroom operations. They get exactly what they need and nothing more.

  • Check in arriving packages
  • Check out packages to residents
  • View their assigned schedule
  • Submit schedule change requests
👤

Assistant Hall Director (AHD)

Mid-level staff who supervise OAs and handle more complex operations and hall administration.

  • All OA capabilities
  • Process RTS and forwarding
  • Manage users in their hall
  • View cross-hall package data
  • Run PO audits and exports
🏛️

Hall Director (HD)

Full administrative control over the entire system, staff, halls, scheduling, and data management.

  • All AHD capabilities
  • Create and manage halls
  • Build and publish shift schedules
  • Duplicate record management
  • Import/export student & package data
